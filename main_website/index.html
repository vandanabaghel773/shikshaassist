About Siksha Assist

Our Mission

We're passionate about empowering students to become confident and effective communicators. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and practical communication skills.

What We Offer

  • AI-Powered Analysis: Our AI analyzes student videos or live speech to assess communication skills like pronunciation, grammar, fluency, and body language.
  • Personalized Feedback: Detailed feedback reports highlight strengths and areas for improvement.
  • Teacher Support: Tools for teachers to monitor and guide student progress.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student can develop exceptional communication skills. By harnessing AI, we aim to make personalized skill development accessible to all.
